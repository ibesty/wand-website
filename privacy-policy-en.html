Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

This policy explains how App MagicWand handles data while providing parental app access management features.

1. Product Scope

App MagicWand is a parental control tool designed to manage app access in structured daily scenarios such as school, home, and bedtime.

2. Official Frameworks

We use Apple's official Screen Time frameworks, including FamilyControls and ManagedSettings, for rule setup and enforcement.

3. No Private APIs

We do not use private APIs. All capabilities are implemented through documented and approved Apple APIs.

4. On-Device Processing

Rule configuration and enforcement are processed locally on the device whenever possible.

5. Data Collection

We do not collect or transmit personal usage data for analytics or advertising.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties, except when required by law or platform-level transaction processing.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Any update will be published on this page with a revised date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@appmagicwand.com.
